# Research

Investigations that inform the design without being decisions themselves —
SOTA surveys, prior-art reviews, and throwaway spikes. A note here may seed a
plan or inform an ADR, but the plan and the ADR are where the forward-looking
record and the settled choice live.

Notes are dated (`YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`). For the wider record this sits in, see
the [development map](../README.md).
